Double cleanse at night

Silk pillowcase for hair

Swap cotton for silk to reduce overnight friction — fewer breakages, less frizz, and smoother skin too.

Layer thinnest to thickest

Apply skincare in order of consistency: toner → serum → moisturizer → oil. Each layer needs to absorb properly.
